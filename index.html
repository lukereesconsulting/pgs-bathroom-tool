Frequently asked questions

Straight answers before you get in touch

How experienced are you?

Christian has been a qualified plumber for 26 years, and has run PGS for about 7 years with a small team plus trusted contractors for bigger jobs.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in East London and cover the surrounding coastal area. Send us a WhatsApp with your suburb or town and we'll confirm we can reach you.

How do I get a quote?

For bathroom renovations, try the free design tool above for an instant estimate. For anything else, message us on WhatsApp with a few details about the job and we'll get back to you.

What are your hours, and do you handle emergencies?

Our regular hours are 08:30–16:30, Monday to Friday. We don't offer after-hours emergency call-outs, but Christian does his best to respond quickly to urgent issues like burst pipes during working hours.
